# Quality – Technical Manifest V4.0 (Public Edition)

> **Module:** Quality Assurance and Observability
> **Parent:** [Technical Manifest](README.md)

---

## Overview

The quality layer makes system behavior measurable, testable, and continuously improvable. It combines live transparency with repeatable validation processes.

---

## 1. Observability Architecture

### Purpose
Transparency over processing, performance, and result quality in real-time.

### Characteristics
- Central collection of metrics, events, and states
- Mission-related segmentation of quality data
- Visibility across all process phases

### Benefits
Early detection of deviations and faster root cause analysis.

---

## 2. Quality Modules

### Source Validation
Checks if answers are consistent with the processed content.

### Consensus Monitoring
Monitors agreement patterns between processing paths.

### Knowledge Validation
Controls consistency and plausibility in knowledge representation.

### Data Integrity Check (Cross-Validation & Provenance)
Validates consistency between semantic components (vector database) and relational facts (knowledge graph). Ensures seamless data provenance and generates fully automated audit reports (Six-Sigma KPI).

### Security and Compliance Monitoring
Monitors policy compliance, access patterns, and data protection aspects.

### Performance Monitoring
Measures throughput, latency, and bottlenecks along the processing chain.

### Benefits
Targeted quality statements instead of blanket overall scores.

---

## 3. Continuous Validation

### Purpose
Asynchronous quality assurance without affecting live operation.

### Components
- Reference dataset-based regression tests
- Challenging test cases for robustness testing
- Trend analysis over repeated test runs

### Benefits
Stable quality across releases, recognizable improvement or deterioration trends.

---

## 4. Learning Loop

### Purpose
Systematic use of error and conflict patterns for optimization.

### Characteristics
- Preparation of recurring weaknesses
- Transfer of relevant cases into test catalogs
- Derivation of prioritized improvement measures

### Benefits
Quality progress becomes predictable and data-based.

---

## Result

The quality layer creates robust transparency for engineering and stakeholders and supports a continuous, traceable improvement process.
