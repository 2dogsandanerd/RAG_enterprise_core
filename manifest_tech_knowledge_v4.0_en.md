# Knowledge – Technical Manifest V4.0 (Public Edition)

> **Module:** Knowledge Management and Retrieval
> **Parent:** [Technical Manifest](README.md)

---

## Overview

The knowledge layer transforms verified document content into a combined structure of semantic context and relational facts. The goal is precise, traceable answers even for complex queries.

---

## 1. Semantic Segmentation

### Purpose
Content-logical division of documents into usable knowledge units.

### Characteristics
- Segmentation along semantic boundaries
- Adaptive segment sizes per content type
- Mission-specific configurability

### Benefits
Better context fidelity and higher hit relevance for search queries.

---

## 2. Entities and Relationships

### Purpose
Extraction of structured facts from documents.

### Characteristics
- Recognition of central entity types and relations
- Linking via document and context references
- Traceable provenance information per fact

### Benefits
Enables fact queries and relationship analyses that pure text search cannot provide.

---

## 3. Query Orchestration

### Purpose
Automatic decomposition of complex questions into manageable subtasks.

### Characteristics
- Recognition of comparison, aggregation, and multi-step questions
- Dynamic selection of suitable retrieval strategies
- Merging sub-results into consistent answers

### Benefits
Stable quality even with demanding questions.

---

## 4. Hybrid Retrieval

### Purpose
Combination of different search principles for higher precision.

### Architecture
- Semantic search for contextual proximity
- Relation-based search for fact-driven precision
- Rank fusion and subsequent relevance check

### Benefits
Fewer false positives, better coverage of professional nuances, higher answer quality.

---

## 5. Answer Stability and Traceability

### Purpose
Robust answers with transparent origin.

### Characteristics
- Consistent linking of answer and evidence
- Evaluation logic for result quality
- Mission-related result control

### Benefits
Increases trust in results and facilitates professional review.

---

## Result

The Knowledge layer provides a robust, extensible basis for precise enterprise retrieval, combining semantic flexibility with fact-oriented reliability.
